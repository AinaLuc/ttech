Frequently Asked Questions

Can I form an LLC in the U.S. as a non-resident?

Yes, you can. The U.S. allows non-residents to own and operate LLCs. Our services are specifically designed to help international entrepreneurs navigate this process.

What are the benefits of forming an LLC?

An LLC provides liability protection, separating your personal assets from your business debts. It also offers tax flexibility and enhances your business's credibility.

How long does the LLC formation process take?

The timeline can vary depending on the state you choose to register in. However, with our streamlined process, we can help you form your LLC in as little as a few business days.
